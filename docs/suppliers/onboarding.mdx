## who can apply?

we're looking for:
- kols with 50k+ followers
- market makers with proven track record
- video producers with viral content
- podcasters with engaged audiences
- newsletter operators with high open rates
- growth agencies with startup clients

## application process

<Steps>
  <Step title="apply">
    fill out the application form with your portfolio and results.
  </Step>
  <Step title="review">
    noice team reviews your application (1-3 business days).
  </Step>
  <Step title="onboarding call">
    if approved, we schedule a call to set up your profile.
  </Step>
  <Step title="profile setup">
    create your supplier page with rates, deliverables, and portfolio.
  </Step>
  <Step title="go live">
    your profile goes live. start accepting quote requests.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## what we look for

### for kols
- follower count and engagement rate
- niche relevance (crypto, startups, tech)
- past promotional work and results
- professional communication

### for video producers
- portfolio of viral content
- production quality
- turnaround time
- pricing structure

### for market makers
- aum and trading volume
- exchange relationships
- past client list
- technical infrastructure

## setting your rates

you set your own rates. consider:
- your reach/audience size
- typical results you deliver
- time investment required
- market rates for similar suppliers

## proof of work

every supplier must provide:
- links to past work
- metrics for each piece (views, engagement)
- client testimonials if available
