## the process

<Steps>
  <Step title="browse">
    explore suppliers by category. look at their results and past work.
  </Step>
  <Step title="request quote">
    click "request quote" on a supplier's page. describe your project.
  </Step>
  <Step title="receive proposal">
    supplier sends you a custom proposal with scope, timeline, and price.
  </Step>
  <Step title="fund escrow">
    approve the proposal and fund with usdc or tokens from your growth wallet.
  </Step>
  <Step title="work begins">
    supplier starts the work. you can communicate through the platform.
  </Step>
  <Step title="approve delivery">
    review the deliverables. approve to release payment.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## payment options

### usdc
pay directly from your growth wallet usdc balance. this comes from trading tax revenue.

### tokens
pay with your growth wallet tokens. suppliers often offer a discount for token payments since they can sell later at higher prices.

## strategy recommendations

noice recommends suppliers based on your wallet balance:

| balance | recommended strategy |
|---------|---------------------|
| < $1k | organic twitter growth |
| $1k - $5k | ghostwriting + engagement |
| $5k - $15k | kol push + trenching |
| $15k+ | market maker + major kols |

## disputes

if a supplier doesn't deliver:
1. raise a dispute in the platform
2. noice team reviews evidence
3. funds returned if supplier failed to deliver
