## prerequisites

- wallet with some eth on base for gas
- company name and one-liner
- twitter handle

## steps

<Steps>
  <Step title="connect wallet">
    go to [noice.so/launch](https://noice.so/launch) and connect your wallet. we support all major wallets.
  </Step>
  <Step title="enter company info">
    fill in your company name, one-liner, twitter handle, and website.
  </Step>
  <Step title="configure token">
    choose your ticker (e.g. $cluely). tokenomics are fixed:
    - 1 billion total supply
    - 2% trading tax (1.6% to you, 0.4% to noice)
    - 50% retail / 40% growth wallet / 10% team
  </Step>
  <Step title="launch">
    review and confirm. your token will be live on base in seconds.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## after launch

### your growth wallet

your growth wallet will start accumulating usdc from trading fees. you can spend this on suppliers in the marketplace.

```
growth wallet balance: $0.00
tokens reserved: 400,000,000
```

### next steps

1. share your token page on twitter
2. browse the [marketplace](/marketplace) for suppliers
3. hire your first kol or video producer
4. track results on your company dashboard
