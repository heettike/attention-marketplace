## before you launch

### prerequisites
- eth on base for gas (~$5)
- company name and branding ready
- twitter account active

### recommended prep
- have some content ready to post on launch day
- line up 2-3 suppliers for day 1 momentum
- brief your community if you have one

## launch checklist

<Checklist>
  - [ ] wallet connected with eth for gas
  - [ ] company name finalized
  - [ ] ticker chosen (check it's not taken)
  - [ ] one-liner ready
  - [ ] twitter and website links
  - [ ] launch tweet drafted
</Checklist>

## launch day

### timeline

| time | action |
|------|--------|
| t-1h | final prep, wallet ready |
| t=0 | deploy token |
| t+5m | share token page on twitter |
| t+1h | first supplier posts (if arranged) |
| t+24h | review day 1 metrics |

### what to post

```
we just launched $TICKER on @noiceapp

[your one-liner]

buy: [link to your token page]

let's see what happens when you can own a piece of attention.
```

## post-launch

### day 1-7
- monitor trading activity
- engage with new holders
- collect trading tax revenue
- plan first supplier hires

### week 2+
- hire first supplier with accumulated revenue
- track results
- iterate on strategy
